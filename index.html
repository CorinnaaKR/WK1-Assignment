Panda Facts

  • Panda poo, because it is primarily made up of bamboo can be made into paper!
  • Giant Pandas are native to China and primarily live in bamboo forests.
  • Giant Pandas are excellent climbers and can reach heights of up to 10,000 feet.
  • Known for their playful nature, they can often be seen rolling around and playing with each other.

The Panda Diet

Giant Pandas are primarily herbivores, with bamboo making up about 99% of their diet. They consume large quantities of bamboo daily, which is low in nutrients, so they need to eat a lot to meet their energy needs.

Giant Pandas also eat other plants, fruits, and on rare occasions. However, is their main and favourite food source.

Due to their specialized diet, Giant Pandas have evolved unique adaptations, such as strong jaws and teeth to help them chew through tough bamboo stalks. They also have a special digestive system that allows them to extract the maximum nutrients from bamboo.
